Hi! I'm Deovam Singh.

I'm a PhD candidate at the University of Maryland, College Park.


I study exoplanet atmospheres, and I generally seek to answer: how does gas move and change in hot gas giants?

I'm trained as a theorist, but I spend the majority of my time "staying close to the data": making predictions, interpreting observations, and as of late developing inference tools. In the coming years, I will apply these tools to data from JWST and the VLT. My interests also span magnetic fields, photochemistry, and code optimization.

Currently, I'm most excited about inferring "3D effects" from hot Jupiters and cross-correlating against JWST data.
